Featured Projects

Arduino Anti-Theft Device

I made this during my first quarter of college. I was brand new to Arduino and Programming but was eager to learn and try new things. The idea behind this project was an anti-theft device one could put on/in their belongings if they had to leave them unattended, like at the library. Once connected to power, the IR remote activates the alarm, initiating a 5 second countdown until the alarm is armed. Once it is armed, if the MPU-6050 senses any movement in the x, y, or z direction, the alarm sounds. Once the alarm sounds, it will keep going until turned off via the IR remote.

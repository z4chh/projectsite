SmartSenty

Utilizes Arduino Microcontroller along with various modules. The IR remote primes the alarm, initiating a 5 second countdown until the alarm is armed. Then, if the accelerometer senses any movement in the x, y, or z direction, the alarm sounds. Once the alarm sounds, it will keep going until turned off via the IR remote. The idea behind this project was an anti-theft device one could put on/in their belongings if they had to leave them unattended, like at the library.
